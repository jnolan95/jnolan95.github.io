Group Project

Over the entirety of the course MGT 6203, students worked on a project. Our group had total control over the project, including picking the dataset, statistical methodologies, and focus of the presentation.

We picked a dataset dealing with the healtcare industry. The dataset had more than 300,000 data points with 15 predictors and 2 response variables.

Using R, we were able to perform a linear regression on all of the predictor variables with Healthcare Cost. We also were able to deploy a Logistic Regression between all of the predictors with a category called "Health Outcome" to determine what role each predictor played in terms of going from a "Healthy" Outcome to the "At-Risk" or "Critical" Outcome categories.
